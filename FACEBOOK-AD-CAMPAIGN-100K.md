## 🗓️ 6-Week Timeline

### **Week 1-2: Testing Phase (30k ISK)**

**Week 1:**
- [ ] Setup Facebook Business Manager
- [ ] Install Facebook Pixel á lioratech.is
- [ ] Create 3 ad variations (A, B, C)
- [ ] Create ad creatives (þarf 3-9 myndir/videos)
- [ ] Launch campaign með 10k ISK/ad (3 ads × 10k = 30k)

**Week 2:**
- [ ] Monitor daily performance (CPM, CTR, CPC, CVR, CPL)
- [ ] Let ads run minimum 48 hours before making changes
- [ ] Identify winning ad (lowest CPL + highest lead quality)

**Success Metrics:**
- ✅ At least 1 ad með CPL < 3.000 ISK
- ✅ Minimum 6 total leads
- ✅ CTR > 1%

**Red Flags:**
- 🚨 CPL > 5.000 ISK á öllum ads
- 🚨 CTR < 0.5%
- 🚨 CVR < 2%

---

### **Week 3-4: Scaling Phase (40k ISK)**

**Week 3:**
- [ ] Pause losing ads (highest CPL)
- [ ] 2x budget á winning ad (20k ISK/week)
- [ ] Create 2 variations af winning ad (different headlines/images)
- [ ] Start retargeting campaign (10k ISK)

**Week 4:**
- [ ] Monitor for ad fatigue (CPM/CPL hækkun)
- [ ] Test broader audience ef CPL er undir 2.500 ISK
- [ ] Create lookalike audience af leads

**Success Metrics:**
- ✅ Maintain CPL < 3.000 ISK við scaling
- ✅ 10-16 new leads
- ✅ Retargeting CVR > 8%

**Red Flags:**
- 🚨 CPL hækkar > 50% við scaling
- 🚨 Frequency > 3 (ad fatigue)
- 🚨 Retargeting ekki að gefa leads

---

### **Week 5-6: Optimization Phase (30k ISK)**

- [ ] Launch lookalike audience campaign (15k ISK)

**Week 6:**
- [ ] Analyze full campaign data
- [ ] Prepare fyrir Month 2 campaign (með $200-300/month)
- [ ] Document learnings (winning ad, best audience, best time)

**Success Metrics:**
- ✅ Lookalike CPL < 3.500 ISK
- ✅ 7-12 total leads
- ✅ Clear winner fyrir Month 2

## 🚨 Red Flags & When to Pause

**Pause ad immediately if:**
1. **CPL > 6.000 ISK** eftir 20 leads - ekki profitable
2. **CTR < 0.3%** eftir 5.000 impressions - creative ekki að virka
3. **CVR < 1%** eftir 100 clicks - landing page issue
4. **Frequency > 4** - ad fatigue, pause og create new creative

**Lower budget if:**
1. CPL 4.000-6.000 ISK - halda áfram en minnka budget
2. Quality score lækkar - Facebook er að throttle adið
